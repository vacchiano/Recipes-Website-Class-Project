Bacon, Eggs & Toast Cups!

  • Ingredients:
  • eggs
  • bread
  • bacon
  • cheese

Step 1: Cook bacon slices in large skillet pan over medium heat for 4-5 minutes.

Step 2: Cut the bread into a circle, around a cup.

Step 3: Lay bread into muffin tin, covering all areas.

Step 4: Stuff cheese and bacon into the bread cups.

Step 5: Crack egg over cheese and bacon.

Step 6: Bake at 375 degrees F for 20-25 minutes.

Ta-Da!
