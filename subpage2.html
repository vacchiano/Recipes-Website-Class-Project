Cheesy Chicken Quesadilla!

  • Ingredients:
  • tortillas
  • chicken
  • cheese
  • onions & peppers

Step 1: Cook seasoned chicken in large skillet pan over medium heat for 4-5 minutes each side.

Step 2: Cut and pan fry onions and peppers.

Step 3: Melt butter in pan.

Step 4: Place tortilla in pan and sprinkle cheese over.

Step 5: Cut the chicken and place on top along with vegetables.

Step 6: Top it off with a little extra cheese.

Step 7: Place other shell on top and flip to fry each side.

Beautiful!
