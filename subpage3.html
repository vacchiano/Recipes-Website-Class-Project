Classic Cheese Burger!

  • Ingredients:
  • ground beef
  • cheese
  • hamburger buns
  • ketchup

Step 1: Flatten 2, 1/4 pound patties of ground beef onto a frying pan.

Step 2: Flip the patties to cook the other side.

Step 3: Place cheese slice ontop of 1 of the patties.

Step 4: Place other burger on top of cheese.

Step 5: Serve on toasted bun with condiments of choice.

Step 6: Yum!.
